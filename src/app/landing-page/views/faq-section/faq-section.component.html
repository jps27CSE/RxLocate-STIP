FAQ

Here are some of the frequently asked questions

How do I perform an effortless location search?

Quickly find marking points on the map that show which locations have prescribed which medicines and how many times.

How can I track prescriptions?

Use the drug search feature to see a list of divisions, detailing how many times each doctor has prescribed a specific drug. With the drug and division search, view district data on a map, showing which doctors prescribed which medications based on division.
